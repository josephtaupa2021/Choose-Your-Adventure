Journal

Bob is a homeless man but one with a beautiful soul. He greets people everyday as they walk pass his fortress made of cupboard boxes and scrap metals, a place he called home. One day, Bob stubbled over an envelope on the side of the road. Inside it he found a bundle of cash worth $1,000,000.
"Today is my lucky day. I've never had this much money in my whole life, and now I'm holding it in my hand", Bob said with tears of joy in his eyes. He hopes to thank whoever left him this big life changing paycheck on the dirty roadside.

Johny Bravo, Street Journals
